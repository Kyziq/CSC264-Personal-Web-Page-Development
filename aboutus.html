ABOUT BURGER MERBOKING

Every day, more than 11 million guests visit BURGER MERBOKING® restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 1954, BURGER MERBOKING® is one of the largest fast food hamburger chain in the world. BURGER MERBOKING® commenced operations in Malaysia in 1997 with the opening of its first restaurant at Merbok, Kedah. Today, Cosmo Restaurants Sdn Bhd operates more than 120 BURGER MERBOKING® restaurants in Malaysia where customers across the country can enjoy the great and healthy flame-grilled taste of Burger MerboKing products. The original HOME OF THE WHOPPER®, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 50 successful years.
